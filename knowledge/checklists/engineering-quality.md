# Engineering Quality Checklist

- Problem statement and constraints are explicit.
- Chosen approach includes rejected alternatives and rationale.
- Naming is clear and behavior-focused.
- Edge cases are documented and covered.
- Complexity tradeoffs are stated.
- Tests or validation steps are captured.
- Reusable pattern is extracted into `knowledge/patterns/` when applicable.
- If the work came from another project, the reusable solution/pattern is still recorded in this repo without waiting for a request.
